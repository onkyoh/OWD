About Me

Onkyoh Web Designs

Helping Small Businesses Grow

I'm Adnan, a web developer and the sole owner of Onkyoh Web Designs. My whole life I have possessed an interest in creating things with technology, so when I stumbled upon web design, there was an instant click. I started this business after seeing too many small businesses with poorly built websites holding back their growth.

I understand many businesses may be reluctant or unable to invest thousands in a website. With this in mind, I've designed a pricing model for small businesses, focusing on long-term growth. Instead of a large upfront cost, I offer a plan with no initial payment and a monthly fee of $150, making it more affordable and reflecting the ongoing value that a quality website can provide.
